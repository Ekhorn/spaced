Current Spaced features

Spaced is a powerful knowledge base that helps you organize and access your information with ease.

  • Whiteboard Interface

    Spaced offers a unique whiteboard-style interface that makes it easy to organize and visualize your knowledge.

  • Multiplatform

    Access your knowledge base from the browser or as desktop app.

  • Multimedia rendering

    Spaced renders pdfs, images, and markdown.
    More coming soon.

  • OCR support

    Utilize AI to extract text from an image. Currently only supported on desktop. Requires manual compilation, see getting started.
